Grey Wolf

Grey wolves have been known to go up to 14 days between meals, with no ill effects.

  • Scientific Name: Canis Iupus
  • Average Height: 82.5cm
  • Average Length: 5.5ft
  • Lifespan: 7-8 years

The gray wolf, also known as the timber wolf or western wolf, is a canine native to the wilderness and remote areas of Eurasia and North America. It is the largest extant member of its family.
